Procedural Planets

WebApp to showcase procedural generation of planets in real time, accelerated by the GPU. Press space to generate new variations of the current planet.

Technologies: WebGL, Javascript, HTML/CSS

Super Resolution GAN

A modified version of the original SRGAN paper by Christian Ledig(https://arxiv.org/pdf/1609.04802.pdf). I changed the loss function to allow it to reach the theoretical limit of 0(impossible in the original paper).

Technologies: WebGL, Javascript, HTML/CSS
